Why pick us?
We moved over $50K for a single client in one quarter through 9 auctions. We know what sells, how to price it, and how to reach the right buyers.
What do you mean by e-commerce management?
We take items from your shop and sell them online to a national audience — through auction platforms like HiBid, eBay, and Whatnot.
How does pricing work?
We work on commission — 10-20% of the sale price when your items sell. No upfront costs, no monthly fees. You only pay when we make you money.
What types of items do you sell?
Coins, collectibles, comics, vintage items, antiques, and estate goods. If it has value to collectors, we can move it.
