How It Works

Three simple steps to turn your inventory into cash.

1

You Send Us Inventory

Drop off or ship us your items. We photograph everything professionally and create optimized listings.

2

Your items go live on HiBid, eBay, and Whatnot. We run targeted campaigns to reach serious collectors nationwide.
